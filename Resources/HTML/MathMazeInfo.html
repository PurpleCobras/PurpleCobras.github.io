Math Maze Information

This maze style game will help the player practice all kinds of mathematical functions in a fun and entertaining way. It is very easy to play and makes solving math problems more fun.

To get started, choose your difficulty:


  • Addition
  • Subtraction
  • Greatest Common Factor


  • Multiplication
  • Division
  • Linear equations with one variable


  • Exponents
  • Least Common Multiple
  • Linear equations with variables on both sides


  • Mix of all Easy, Moderate, and Hard problems

Choose the color of the player box:







The equation you must solve is shown to the left of the maze.

Use the arrow keys to navigate through the maze.



There are 4 possible answers to the equation. Choose the correct answer quickly to get the most points and move to the next question.

If the chosen answer is correct, +1 will be added to the left side of the visualization counter:



There are 4 possible answers to the equation. Choose the correct answer quickly to get the most points and move to the next question.

If the chosen answer is incorrect, +1 will be added to the right side of the visualization counter:



When you have chosen the correct answer, points are awarded to you based on the difficulty of the question and how quickly you solved it.
