Math Maze Information

This maze style game will help the player practice all kinds of mathematical functions in a fun and entertaining way. It is very easy to play and makes solving math problems more fun.

The first thing you do is choose the difficulty you want to play at. You then are able to choose the color of the player box in the game. When the maze loads, you are given a math problem to solve. The problems come in many forms based on the difficulty. There are some that ask for the greatest common factor, some that require a multistep solving process, and some that are just simple addition and subtraction.

First the player must solve the problem given. Then the player must find the correct answer in the maze. There are 4 answers placed on the for each problem. If the player retrieves a wrong answer the max possible score decreases and the miss count increases. The amount of possible points depends on the difficulty as well as the time that has elapsed. Once the player has retrieved the correct answer, points are awarded depending on difficulty and time and the hit count increases. There is a visualization for a correct and wrong answer. If the answer chosen is wrong a red 'x' floats up and the miss box in the visualization is made red for a few seconds. If the answer chosen is correct a green 'check' floats up and the hit box in the visualization is made green for a few seconds. A different problem with a different maze is generated and loaded to the screen.

Difficulties:
  • Easy
    • Addition
    • Subtraction
    • Greatest Common Factor
  • Moderate
    • Multiplication
    • Division
    • Linear equations with one variable
  • Hard
    • Exponents
    • Least Common Multiple
    • Linear equations with variables on both sides
  • All
    • Mix of all Easy, Moderate, and Hard problems
