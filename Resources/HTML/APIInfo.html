2) Visualize the speeds of the gates.

  • The speed for each gate is randomly selected when it begins to fall.
  • Use rotating dice to visualize the random selection for the speeds.
  • Each bar corresponds to one gate.
  • Use the lengths of the bars to represent the speeds of the gates.
  • The longer the bar is, the faster the gate is moving.
  • For example, the left bar represents the speed of the leftmost gate.
