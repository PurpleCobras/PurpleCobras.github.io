Our Story

The Purple Cobras are a group of five Senior Computer Science students at Ohio University. The games and visualizations throughout this site were developed for a Senior Design Capstone project over the course of 2 semesters.

Our main goal is to create fun games and helping you learn while playing them. You can practice your math skills with Math Maze or learn about Boolean Logic Gates with Gate Grabber!

Check out our games and leave your feedback on the Gate Grabber Info page or the Math Maze Info page!
